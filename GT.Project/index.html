HEIDI

written by Paul Martin

Heidi was a small girl lived with her aunt in Sweden.

She was the 1stchild of the Vermadore family.

Her grandfather lived on the mountains,he was ver weak and old

One day she went to meet her grandfather. He was very happy to see her and insisted to
keep her with him.

Aunt was worried because she knew that grandfather is too old to take care of her but grandfather said I miss her so much.

Heidi was happy with grandfather. here is a boy named PETER who takes care of grandfather and his goats.

Peter's grandmother was blind. Heidi tells her about the beauty of mountains.

One day Heidi was telling the formula of H2O to the Peter while she saw a rolling stone.......which can hit Peter
